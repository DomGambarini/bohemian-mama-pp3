ABOUT

Nestled along the rugged coastline of Cornwall, Bohemian Mama is a food blog born out of the culinary magic spun by a Cornish grandma with an unbridled passion for local and foraged produce. Drawing inspiration from the rich tapestry of Cornwall's landscapes, this blog is a testament to the love and respect for seasonal ingredients, many of which are lovingly grown in her own garden.

Our Culinary Philosophy:

At Bohemian Mama, we believe that every meal should tell a story. Whether it's the earthy sweetness of a freshly plucked apple or the robust flavor of foraged mushrooms, each ingredient has its own narrative to share. Our recipes are a celebration of these stories, weaving together the tapestry of Cornish flavors into delectable, wholesome dishes.

From Garden to Table:

One of the cornerstones of Bohemian Mama's kitchen is the bountiful garden that serves as the heart of our culinary creations. Here, every herb, vegetable, and fruit is cultivated with care and intention, ensuring that each bite carries the essence of Cornish terroir. Join us on a journey from seed to plate, where the love for gardening meets the joy of cooking.

Foraged Finds and Local Treasures:

Bohemian Mama takes pride in sourcing ingredients from the local community and the untamed beauty of Cornwall. From wild berries gathered on coastal walks to mushrooms discovered in hidden woodland nooks, our recipes showcase the unique flavors that emerge when nature and culinary artistry collide.

Your Culinary Companion:

Become a part of the Bohemian Mama community by registering on our site. We believe in the joy of cooking and the power of collective culinary creativity, and we invite you to be a part of this vibrant kitchen.
